Electric Charges & Fields

The "Soul" of Matter. Comprehensive Notes for Toppers.

1. The Nature of Charge

Charge is an intrinsic property. Like mass, but can be positive or negative.

The Memory Story: "The Franklin Party"
Imagine Benjamin Franklin hosting a party. When a Glass Rod danced with Silk, the Glass got tired (lost electrons, became +ve), and Silk gained energy (gained electrons, became -ve). The total number of guests never changed (Conservation)!

4. Electric Dipole

A "Power Couple" (+q and -q). Net Force is zero, but they spin (Torque).

The Memory Story: "The Dancing Duo"
Positive and Negative hold hands. They refuse to move from their spot (Force=0), but when the music starts (Electric Field), they twist and turn (Torque) to face the audience!

Total Flux depends ONLY on enclosed charge.
