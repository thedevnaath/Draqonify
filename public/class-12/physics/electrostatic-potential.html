Electrostatic Potential and Capacitance

Scalar Side of Electricity.

2.1 Electrostatic Potential

Work done to bring unit positive charge from infinity.
$$V = W/q_0$$
Conceptify Imagine a hill. Ground is zero. Pushing rock up is work. Higher you go, more potential.

2.2 Potential due to Point Charge

Derivation
$$V = kQ/r$$

2.3 Dipole Potential

Derivation
$$V = kp \cos\theta / r^2$$
Conceptify On equator, you are equal distance from Good Twin (+) and Bad Twin (-). Pulls cancel. Zero Potential.

2.4 Equipotential Surfaces

Surface with same potential everywhere. Work done is zero.

2.5 Potential Energy

$$U = k q_1 q_2 / r$$

2.6 Conductors

  1. Inside E = 0.
  2. Surface E is perpendicular.
  3. Interior Potential is constant.

2.7 Dielectrics

Conceptify Shouting through a pillow. Pillow (Dielectric) absorbs sound (Field).

2.8 Capacitors

$$C = Q/V$$
Conceptify Capacitor is a Bucket. Charge is water. Voltage is pressure. Capacitance is width.

2.9 Parallel Plate

Derivation
$$C = \epsilon_0 A / d$$

2.10 Effect of Dielectric

$$C = K \epsilon_0 A / d$$
Conceptify Sponge between plates weakens field, allows more charge storage.

2.11 Combination

Series$$1/C = 1/C_1 + 1/C_2$$
Parallel$$C = C_1 + C_2$$

2.12 Energy Stored

$$U = 1/2 CV^2$$

Remember Forever Story

Potential is Height. Capacitor is Bucket. Dielectric is Sponge.
